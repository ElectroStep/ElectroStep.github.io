What is ElectroStep?

ElectroStep is an add-on that can fit on almost any shoe. It allows the user to generate electricity while walking. The aim of this project is to fight against climate change by encouraging people to walk instead of using cars!

How ElectroStep works?

ElectroStep relies on piezoelectric plates to convert your steps into electricity. The electricity is converted from AC to DC and the direct current is then used to charge the batteries on the outer side of the shoe.

What went wrong:

ElectroStep was initially made using cardboard, for the base, and styrofoam, to apply pressure on the piezoelectric plates. However, carboard was too thick and overall, it proved to be very uncomfortable for the user. After a few google searches and some help, I found that the best material for ElectroStep were these thin foam sheets. They were both better-looking and much more comfortable than using cardboard.
